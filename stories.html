Conjoined Fingers Separated — 14-Year-Old Girl Regains Full Hand Function

👧 Gopika, 14 · Anthiyur, Erode · Syndactyly Release · May 2025

Gopika from Anthiyur had her fingers fused together from birth — a condition called syndactyly — affecting both hands. She was admitted to the Surgery Department on 10.05.2025. On 13.05.2025, our plastic surgery team successfully separated the index and middle fingers of her right hand. The remaining fingers will be separated in a follow-up procedure after three months.

"A surgery that would have cost ₹80,000–1,00,000 in a private hospital was performed completely free under the CM's Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme."

Brain Infection After Accident — 4-Hour Neurosurgery Restores Life

👨 Ilayaraja, 44 · Jeevanandham Road, Erode · Neurosurgery · July 2023

Ilayaraja had sustained a head injury in an accident two years prior and undergone surgery at Salem Government Medical College. After six months of recurring pus and fever at the surgical site, scans revealed the skull bone had developed osteomyelitis — a bone infection — pressing on the brain. On 12.07.2023, Dr. Sayi Kali Sanjeev (Neurosurgery), Dr. Ramya Kannan (Anaesthesia), and nurse Vaidehi performed a 4-hour surgery, removing the infected bone and clearing the pus from the brain surface. He was discharged after 5 days of IV antibiotics, fully recovered.

Emergency Surgery Saved ₹5 Lakh

Severed Hand Reattached in 9-Hour Emergency Surgery After Rail Accident

👨 Mahalingam, 52 · P.B.Agragharam, Erode · Hand Replantation · May 2024

Mahalingam met with an unexpected accident on 30.05.2024, losing his right hand completely. He was rushed to the Emergency Department at 9:30 PM the same night. Acting immediately, the surgical team decided on emergency replantation. He was taken to the operation theatre at 12:30 AM, anaesthesia was administered at 1:30 AM, and Dr. Gowdham led a 9-hour marathon surgery with 3 nursing staff and 4 support personnel — successfully reattaching the severed hand. He was monitored in the hospital for 15 days, after which he was discharged with full arm function restored.

Oncology + Spine Saved ₹2.5 Lakh

Cancer Spread to Spine — Emergency Surgery Relieves Pain, Restores Walking

👨 Selvaraj, 54 · Kadachanallur, Namakkal · Spinal Neurosurgery · 2025

Selvaraj was diagnosed with cancer three months earlier and was undergoing chemotherapy at Erode Government Hospital. He suddenly developed severe back pain and difficulty walking. CT scan and blood tests confirmed the cancer had spread to the spine, compressing the spinal cord. Emergency neurosurgery was performed under general anaesthesia to completely remove the tumour pressing on the spinal cord. Post-surgery, his back pain vanished completely and he began walking normally. He was monitored at the hospital for 15 days before discharge.

Urology Saved Over ₹1 Lakh

Cancerous Kidney Removed in 74-Year-Old — Radical Nephrectomy Success

👴 Jayapal, 74 · Pallipalayam, Erode · Radical Nephrectomy · July 2025

Jayapal, a 74-year-old from Pallipalayam, was admitted to the Urology Department with a cancerous tumour in his left kidney. Blood tests also revealed anaemia, so blood transfusions were given to stabilise him before surgery. Dr. R. Vinothprabhu (MS, MCh Urology) and the surgical team then performed a Left Radical Nephrectomy, completely removing the affected kidney. The excised tumour was sent for histopathological examination (HPE). Jayapal was closely monitored for 10 days post-surgery and discharged in good health.

6,600+ Patients Receive Specialised Pain Relief — Free — Since 2018

🏥 Pain & Palliative Care Centre · Erode GH · Est. 2018 · Jan–Oct (Current Year)

The Pain and Palliative Care Centre at Erode Government Headquarters Hospital has been operating since 2018, providing relief to cancer patients and those with other chronic illnesses. From January to October this year alone, the centre served 5,197 cancer patients and 1,424 patients with other chronic conditions — all receiving specialised pain medications supplied free by the government.

Beyond pain relief, the centre treats breathlessness in heart, lung, and liver disease patients; manages insomnia and body swelling; and provides Tracheostomy, Colostomy, and Lymphoedema care.
